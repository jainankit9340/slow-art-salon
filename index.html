About Us

Slow art is a cultural movement with a simple mission - to find the joy of doing things at the right pace.

Why slow? The Slow philosophy is not about doing everything at a snail's pace, it’s more than meet the eye. It’s about making discoveries, it’s about enjoying the process and the journey. It is against the notion that faster is always better.

Slow is beautiful. Slowing down unlocks one’s senses and creativity. It makes you realise new and exciting ways of understanding art.

The salon - At Slow art, we look to quiet the pace of the perfect salon experience and change the way we approach grooming. We believe in taking time, slowing down, and crafting the best makeover for you. Our salon has been designed with a modern twist combining chic and elegant interiors with state-of-the-art technology. With a wide variety of services, talented hairstylists, and skin therapists as per global standards - our offering aims at creating an unrivalled luxury experience for the clients.

So the next time you visit our salon, slow down and savour the experience!
